1. #FND-05 — Engine await promises (<20 LOC)
─────────────────────────────────────────────
  File: impl/control/src/workflow/engine.ts

  Current: executeLoop has `await sleep(100)` polling when nodes are running.
  Fix: await the Promise.all of running node promises directly instead
  of sleeping. Keep polling as crash-recovery fallback only.

  Approach: the promises from node execution are already tracked in
  executeLoop. Replace the sleep with awaiting those promises. The loop
  already handles timeouts via Promise.race.
