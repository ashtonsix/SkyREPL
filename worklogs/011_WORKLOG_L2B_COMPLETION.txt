STRATEGY: Destabilize-First Sequencing
================================================================================

Principle: do the most destabilizing work first while spec/ and impl/ are still
"rough." This prevents "stabilize → immediately destabilize again" frustration.
Structural moves happen before correctness fixes. Fresh audit follows fixes.

  Phase 1 — DISTRIBUTION  (move content out of spec/)
  Phase 2 — STRUCTURE     (stabilize references, trim L1)
  Phase 3 — CORRECTNESS 1 (fix all known issues from current discovery)
  Phase 4 — CORRECTNESS 2 (fresh audit, catch regressions + new issues)
  Phase 5 — TRANSITION    (plan the move to working code)
