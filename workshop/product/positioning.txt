# Competitive Positioning

SkyREPL is part of the Sky Computing ecosystem. SkyPilot focuses on cluster
management and job scheduling; SkyREPL takes a complementary approach,
emphasizing lifecycle management and reproducibility for development workflows.

Current support: AWS, Lambda Labs, RunPod, OrbStack.
Target ecosystem: +GCP, Azure, CoreWeave, Vast.ai, SLURM.
