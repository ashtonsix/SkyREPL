The cloud was built for long-lived services, but the AI era is defined by ephemeral intensity. This mismatch forces a choice between velocity and budget safety, a crisis exacerbated by the move toward Sky Computing. As the market shifts toward a global commodity model governed by price arbitrage, the sheer complexity of navigating fragmented cloud offerings has moved beyond what manual oversight can cover. Without a hardened safety layer, this complexity inevitably produces a graveyard of $100/hour "zombie instances."

SkyREPL ends this chaos by hardening the infrastructure lifecycle into a predictable, invisible utility. We achieve this through a "Physical Integrity" guarantee: by treating Allocations as a primary primitive, every resource is bound to an Ownership Manifest that tracks its lifecycle from spawn to destruction. This architecture is orphan-proof by design, providing the necessary rigor for an agentic era where scripts and AI agents must autonomously churn through massive infrastructure across multiple providers.

This structural integrity enables a fundamental shift toward Accountable Compute. For the solo researcher, it means the freedom to spin up H100s at the speed of thought without the mental tax of manual cleanup. As these workflows scale, SkyREPL provides the guardrails for teams to drive massive infrastructure, ensuring that autonomous scripts and junior engineers cannot trigger financial catastrophe. Scaling further, our governance rigor allows the enterprise to treat Budget as the ACL. By transforming infrastructure into a Transparent Audit Trail, SkyREPL provides the line-item attribution and economic control needed to ensure compute is always routed exactly where it creates the most value.
