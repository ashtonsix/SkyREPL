## Project Meta-Ontology (Restructuring)

The current "impl vs. spec" split has reached its limit. I propose a new top-level structure:

- `impl/` (formerly `v2/`): Contains all L1, L2, and L3 pseudo-implementation files, and eventually, the implementation itself.
- `docs/`: Contains the formal core specification and all supporting documentation, including reference docs, usage docs, and other kinds of docs.
- `workshop/`: A dedicated space for non-technical planning, including product scope, UX design, commercial strategy, branding, release plans, and social outreach.

Take this proposal as a starting point only: dispatch an Opus agent, tasking it with proposing something better (once you have gathered enough context to provide it with working material).
