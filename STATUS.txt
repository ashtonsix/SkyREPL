1. OVERVIEW
================================================================================

SkyREPL is a cloud compute orchestration platform for running computational
workloads on ephemeral compute instances. Part of the Sky Computing ecosystem
(SkyPilot as prominent touchstone), SkyREPL focuses on lifecycle management.

Key insight: Cloud orchestration isn't a compute problem - it's a lifecycle
chaos problem. Tracking what's running, ensuring cleanup, avoiding cost surprises.
