# control.types.ts - Control Plane Types

## Config
- `ControlPlaneConfig` - dbPath, port, s3Bucket, s3Endpoint?, tailscaleAuthKey?, logLevel, enableMetrics?, enableTracing?

## Services
- `WorkflowEngineService` - submit, get, getProgress, cancel, pause, resume, retry, registerExecutor, registerBlueprint, subscribe
- `ProviderRegistry` - register, get, list, registerHook, triggerHook
- `Provider` - Interface: name, spawn, get, terminate, createSnapshot, listSnapshots, deleteSnapshot, validateCredentials, getRegions

## Background Tasks
- `BackgroundTask` - name, interval, fn, lastRun, running
- `BackgroundTaskRunner` - start, stop, forceRun, getStatus

## WebSocket & SSE
- `WebSocketConnection` - id, runId?, workflowId?, send, close, isOpen
- `SSEConnection` - id, instanceId?, send, close, isOpen
- `WebSocketManager` - connections, subscribe, unsubscribe, broadcast
- `SSEManager` - connections, subscribe, unsubscribe, sendCommand

## Health
- `HealthStatus` - status, version, uptime, database, storage, tailscale, providers[], backgroundTasks[]
