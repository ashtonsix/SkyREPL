# artifacts.py - Artifact Collection

## Collection
- Glob pattern matching from StartRunMessage.artifacts
- Collect files matching patterns from workdir

## Upload
- Upload to presigned URLs (S3 or control plane for <4KB)
- SHA256 checksum calculation
- Send ArtifactMessage confirmation after upload

## ArtifactMessage
- type, run_id, path, s3_key, size_bytes

## Spot Interruption
- Best-effort upload after checkpoint script
- Continue even if some uploads fail
