# types.ts - Cross-cutting Type Definitions

Cross-cutting primitives and utilities.
See L2 for InstanceSpec normalization, WorkflowState parsing, Duration/Timestamp utilities, pre-defined workflow state builders.

## Primitives

ID (number), TimestampMs (number), DurationMs (number), Checksum (string), ProviderName (aws | lambda | runpod | orbstack)

## IDUtilities

idToSlug(id), slugToId(slug), formatId(id)
Convert integer IDs to/from base-36 slugs for display

## InstanceSpec

instanceType, vcpus, memoryMb, gpuCount?, gpuType?, storageMb?, spot?
Normalized view across providers

## StatusEnums

AllocationStatus: AVAILABLE | CLAIMED | ACTIVE | COMPLETE | FAILED
WorkflowExecutionStatus: pending | running | completed | failed | cancelled | rolling_back
ManifestStatus: DRAFT | SEALED (EXPIRED is derived)
NodeStatus: pending | running | completed | failed | skipped
TailscaleStatus: not_installed | installing | ready | failed

## WorkflowState

Format: "workflow:phase"
Availability is INFERRED: workflowState.endsWith(':complete') && currentManifestId === null
parseStatus(status), buildWorkflowState(workflow, phase), isAvailable(resource)

## Domain Resources

Instance, Run, Allocation, Workflow, Manifest
Fields include: id, status/workflowState, timestamps, foreign keys, currentManifestId

Allocation.debugHoldUntil: Preserves COMPLETE allocation for debugging, max ABSOLUTE_MAX_HOLD_MS (24h)

## Simple Resources

Snapshot, Artifact, FeatureInstallation, TailscaleMachine
Object-backed, simpler lifecycle

## SupportingTypes

RunFile, RunLogEntry, UsageRecord, WorkflowNode, ManifestResource
ManifestResource includes ownerType/ownerId for provenance tracking

## Utilities

Duration: SECOND/MINUTE/HOUR/DAY, seconds/minutes/hours/days(n), format(ms), parse(str)
Timestamp: now(), fromDate(date), toDate(ts), addMs(ts, ms), isExpired(expiresAt)
